## Part I: Open Source Scientific Evidence

### Source 1: Zhineng Qigong for Chronic Low Back Pain

**Citation:** Pozarek G, Strömqvist B, Ekvall Hansson E, Ahlström G. Pain and function in patients with chronic low back pain and leg pain after Zhineng Qigong – a quasi-experimental feasibility study. BMC Musculoskelet Disord. 2023 Jun 13;24:480.

**Open Access:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10262421/

**License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)

**Key Findings:**
- 52 patients with chronic pain (mean 15 years symptom duration)
- 12-week intervention: 78 hours group training + 14 min daily individual practice
- Statistically significant improvements in:
  - Pain reduction
  - Oswestry Disability Index (function)
  - SF-36 quality of life (all scales)
  - EQ-5D-5L health scores
- Even post-surgical patients (1-6 years after lumbar surgery) improved

**Core Principle — The Open System:**

> "Zhineng Qigong is an open style practice. This means that from the beginning, one opens one's mind and qi to the external world and exchanges qi with it. This contrasts with the closed style of most traditional qigong, where the practitioner focuses inside the body for much of their practice."

**Key Findings:**

These "three regulations" (三調) form the foundation of practice.

**Core Principle — The Self-Healing Mechanism:**

(2010):* The researchers describe a traditional teaching: when the practitioner attends to bodily alignment and breath, then quiets the mind, an internal state of coherence arises. This integrated state — body aligned, breath flowing, mind clear — activates what the authors call the "natural self-regulatory (self-healing) capacity." The body, when properly attended to, knows how to heal itself.

## Part VI: Practice Instructions

### La Qi (拉气) — Opening and Closing

The fundamental rhythm of Zhineng Qigong:

1. **Opening (开):** Hands move apart, mind expands outward, qi gathers from universe
2. **Closing (合):** Hands move together, mind focuses inward, qi condenses into body
3. **Repeat:** The rhythm continues, each cycle deepening the integration

This is the **Breath Loop** in physical form:
- Inhale = Opening (receiving)
- Exhale = Closing (integrating)
- Loop = Continuous exchange with universe

### Lift Qi Up, Pour Qi Down (捧气贯顶法)

The first and most fundamental Zhineng Qigong method:

1. **Lift Qi Up:** Gather hunyuan qi from the universe, raise hands and intention upward
2. **Pour Qi Down:** Allow qi to descend through the crown, fill the body, settle in the lower dantian (丹田)

This is the **vertical axis** of qi flow, complementing the horizontal opening-closing of La Qi.
