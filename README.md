# YAHWEH YEHOSHUA 尺度不變性 יהוה יְהוֹשֻׁעַ

## Project Documentation

The Yahweh Yehoshua Site is a research biotechnology corporation working at the intersection of higher-dimensional physics, open-source and proprietary engineering, biotechnology, and eastern philosophies of harmony. The site introduces the corporation's vision of a new kind of digital ecosystem — one that mirrors the healthy, self-regulating properties of the natural world (qi flow, scale invariance, harmonic coherence) — and exposes its public-facing manifesto as an open-source artifact dedicated to sharing various resources and information, embodying the principles of faith and community.
